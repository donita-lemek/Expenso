ACME CORP TRAVEL & EXPENSE POLICY v3.2

SECTION 1 – MEALS & DINING
1.1 Daily meal limits per city tier:
  Tier A (New York, London, San Francisco, Tokyo):
    Breakfast $20 | Lunch $30 | Dinner $60
  Tier B (Chicago, Boston, Austin, Seattle):
    Breakfast $15 | Lunch $25 | Dinner $45
  Tier C (All other cities):
    Breakfast $10 | Lunch $20 | Dinner $30
1.2 Alcohol is NOT reimbursable under any circumstances.
1.3 Team meals require minimum 3 attendees.
1.4 Weekend meals require prior manager approval unless client-facing.
1.5 Tips reimbursable up to 18% of pre-tax bill.

SECTION 2 – TRANSPORT
2.1 Economy class airfare only. Business class requires VP approval.
2.2 Rideshare reimbursable for business travel only. Personal commutes excluded.
2.3 Mileage rate: $0.67/mile.
2.4 Airport parking reimbursable for trips > 2 days. Valet parking NOT reimbursable.
2.5 Economy or compact car rentals only.

SECTION 3 – LODGING
3.1 Hotel nightly limits:
    Tier A $350 | Tier B $250 | Tier C $175
3.2 Minibar, in-room movies, personal laundry NOT reimbursable.

SECTION 4 – ENTERTAINMENT
4.1 Client entertainment limit: $150 per client.
4.2 Sporting events and concerts require VP approval.
4.3 Team building must be pre-approved, business day only.
4.4 Client gifts: max $50 per client per year.

SECTION 5 – GENERAL
5.1 Submit within 30 days of transaction.
5.2 Receipts required for expenses above $25.
5.3 All expenses must have documented business purpose.
